## 2. Module 1 — Economic & Market Principles (15 hours)

**Purpose (WMI):** Build a practical template for understanding how economies and markets work, the forces that drive them, and how asset prices respond.

### 2.1 Published topic headings (ADGM Academy)

1. **A Template for Understanding How the Economic Machine Works**
2. **Anticipating, Managing, and Trading Debt Cycles**
3. **Paradigm Shifts and Changing World Orders**

### 2.2 Concepts covered within these topics (WMI)

- Key forces driving economic swings and the important equilibriums
- The distinction between **money and credit**
- Economic drivers of **asset returns** and how asset prices reflect them
- **Short-term debt cycle** mechanics (business cycle)
- **Long-term debt cycle** mechanics
- **Deflationary** and **inflationary** deleveragings
- Policymaker responses and the resulting trading opportunities
- **Paradigm shifts** — large shifts in how markets and economies operate
- **Currency value dynamics** and the history of reserve currencies
- The rise and fall of nations / changing world orders

---

## 3. Module 2 — Investment Principles (15 hours)

**Purpose (WMI):** Teach Ray Dalio's investment methodology and give participants hands-on experience building and stress-testing portfolios.

### 3.1 Published topic headings (ADGM Academy)

1. **A Template for Investing**
2. **Principles for Building the Best Beta and Alpha Portfolios**
3. **Stress-Testing Portfolios and Investment Ideas**

### 3.2 Concepts covered within these topics (WMI)

- **Core tenets of Ray's distinctive approach:** "fundamental, systematic, and diversified"
- Principles for **selecting and engineering return streams**
- **Portfolio construction** and navigating different investment risks
- Engineering the best **passive (beta) portfolios**, including an in-depth look at the principles guiding the construction of the **All-Weather Portfolio**
- **Alpha generation** principles
- Building and **stress-testing robust beta and alpha portfolios** using a practical investment framework grounded in Ray Dalio's principles
- Hands-on practice with the proprietary **Portfolio Simulator** across varied economic environments

## 4. Learning technologies and components

- **Generative AI Tutor** — personalised coaching that can "answer questions, generate quizzes, provide feedback, and search through Dalio's writings over the past decades"
- **Portfolio Simulator** — build and stress-test portfolios using historical data spanning "more than 40 major economies" over approximately 100–120 years; case-study data current through 2023
- Videos and animations
- Quizzes to reinforce concepts
- Case studies based on "actual historical data, including data up to 2023"
- Discussion forums
- Optional live workshops (ADGM track: four virtual sessions)
