## Summary

**Overall confidence: LOW.** The report passes structural and attribution-presence checks, the palette is clean, the URLs in § 2 for the "The All Weather Story" source are verbatim-accurate, and the weights quote from the tonyrobbins.com reprint is verbatim-accurate. **But four distinct substance failures force REJECT:**

1. **Arithmetic is wrong.** The § 7 worked example's Step-3 per-sleeve risk contributions are not what the stated inputs produce. This is not a rounding issue — the RCs sum to 108.1%, and each individual (Σw)_i differs from the correct value by a factor of 2-4×. This contaminates Step 4, § 8c's chart data, and the "growth down / inflation down is bond-heavy" narrative conclusion. A portfolio manager plugging these numbers into a spreadsheet would get different results and lose confidence in the whole framework.
2. **The Vanguard drift-band source is dead (404) AND the quoted text is fabricated-as-verbatim AND the 3% band is not in the real paper.** Three independent CRITICAL failures on one citation. The real 2015 Zilbering paper discusses 1%/5%/10%, never 3%. The GREEN/AMBER 3% cutoff is the author's own choice dressed up as Vanguard-sourced.
3. **Three Wave-0-retro-flagged slips are still present** (±25%, 1.5×, +1/0/−1 matrix), each MAJOR under R7b. Plus a fourth previously-unnoticed ±25% vol band in § 6 line 97.
4. **Publication year wrong (2022 vs real 2015)**, compounding the misattribution.

**Biggest residual risk:** the § 7 arithmetic error looks like a calculation done by hand and never re-verified. Whatever re-spawn the main session does, the worked example MUST be recomputed from first principles with either (a) a committed script producing the numbers, or (b) a different, simpler example the author hand-verifies. Anything less recurs this bug.
